About Us
Shiyuba Solutions Inc is an Information Technology Company that is dedicated to
providing white label, fully customizable digital ticketing and service voucher
solutions capable of processing digital services coupons and traceable vouchers for
service-oriented commercial transactions. Shuyiba aims to provide its clients with
competitive advantages, using voucher solutions and strategic social media marketing
to help build a solid customer base.
